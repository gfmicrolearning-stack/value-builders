Three principles. Non-negotiable.

Everything published here passes the same test: can a commercial team use it in their next pricing cycle? If not, it doesn't ship.

Principle 1

Value first, cost never

Price is a function of the value delivered — €/ha saved, hours freed, risk avoided — not of the cost incurred. Cost-plus hands your strategy to the accounting department. We start from customer outcomes and willingness-to-pay, every time.

Principle 2

AI as co-pilot, not autopilot

No dynamic pricing hype. No "let the algorithm decide." AI prepares the analysis — competitor scans, scenario models, value calculations — and the pricing professional makes the call. Use it, verify it, own the output.

Principle 3

Data → So-what → Action

Numbers alone are noise. Insight alone is academic. Every method here comes with the data behind it, what it means, and what to do Monday morning — including the don'ts, not just the dos.

About
